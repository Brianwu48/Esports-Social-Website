About

The esports program at Farmingdale college is a thriving organization with 6 teams and over 150 students. We are an all accepting program that allows not only competitive but recreational players as well. Although we allow all students to be apart of our club we thrive to be a winning organization. We teach skills not only within each game but skills you can use throughout life. Here at Farmingdale State Esports we are a family.
